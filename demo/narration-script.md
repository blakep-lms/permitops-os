# PermitOps OS V4 Narration — Hackathon Optimized

Permit consulting is a billion-dollar industry run on spreadsheets, email chains, and sticky notes. a permit consulting business is a real permit consulting business in California. Pearl is their AI operator, built on Hermes Agent.

Here's how Pearl earns, spends, and runs operations.

A new client request comes in. Pearl classifies the case, links the person, business entity, premises, city, and license type. She checks packet readiness against city requirements — what's ready, what's missing, what needs human review.

This is where Pearl starts earning. She drafts a service invoice through Stripe and sends it for client payment. When the client pays, Pearl confirms receipt and moves the case forward. Real revenue, tracked end to end.

Pearl also spends. When a case requires a city filing fee, OCR processing, or a background check API, Pearl queues the expense through Stripe with a reason and a limit. A human approves before any money moves. The agent handles the workflow. The human keeps authority.

That approval gate is the product. Pearl drafts every document, checks every packet, and prepares every filing — but she does not send, file, move private folders, or spend money without sign-off. The safety layer isn't a limitation. It's why regulated businesses can actually use this.

The operating pattern runs on Hermes Agent with NVIDIA-accelerated inference, Stripe Skills for payments, and a memory layer that learns each client's workflow over time.

PermitOps is the first vertical — permit consultants. The same pattern becomes FieldOps for contractors, ServiceOps for local service businesses, and a back-office command layer for any team that needs more than another chatbot.

This isn't a demo. It's a real operator, running real operations, inside a real business. PermitOps OS, built on Hermes Agent.

Earn. Spend. Operate. Approval-gated. Human-authority-preserved.
